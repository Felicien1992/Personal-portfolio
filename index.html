About Me

My name is Felicien MUKESHARUGAMBA. I was born in Burundi, and I am currently 31 years old. I now reside in Rwanda within the Mahama refugee camp. In 2014, I successfully completed my high school education with a focus on IT Maintenance. I'm excited to share that I am one of the students who have been granted the opportunity to study at Alight Mahama Coding School. Through this program, I've acquired a diverse skill set, including proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I continue to expand my knowledge base in various other areas. As a frontend developer, I am eager to apply my skills and expertise to create dynamic and engaging web applications. I am actively seeking new opportunities in the field of frontend development, and I'm excited to contribute my skills to projects that will allow me to grow as a developer and make a meaningful impact.
